Recon workflow
1
Port scan: nmap -sC -sV -p- -T4 target
2
Dir brute: gobuster dir -u http://target -w medium.txt -x php,html,txt
3
DNS: dig ANY target.com then zone transfer
4
Subdomains: amass enum -passive -d target.com
5
Check WHOIS, robots.txt, /.git/, /.env, /backup/

ROP chain building steps
1
Run checksec, confirm NX enabled
2
Find gadgets: ROPgadget --binary ./vuln | grep "pop rdi"
3
Find /bin/sh: strings -t x ./libc | grep /bin/sh
4
Build chain: padding + pop_rdi + binsh_addr + system_addr
5
Add ret gadget before system for 16-byte stack alignment

Quick Tips

The habits that separate fast solves from hours of frustration.

When stuck
1
Re-read the challenge title and description, hints are hidden there
2
Run strings, file, exiftool first
3
Paste into CyberChef Magic mode, it auto-detects most encodings
4
Search: "[category] [keyword] CTF writeup"
5
Hit the ? button to explain any command you are unsure about
6
Take a break. Fresh eyes solve more than frustration does.
